ABOUT ME

I'm Ekezie Uzochukwu Richmond, a Backend Data Engineer specializing in building robust, scalable data infrastructure and backend systems. My expertise spans across data modeling, database design and migration, ETL pipeline development, and backend service architecture.

I architect and implement efficient data solutions that transform raw data into actionable insights, working extensively with relational and NoSQL databases, data warehouses, and modern ETL frameworks. My approach combines software engineering best practices with data engineering principles to deliver reliable, maintainable systems.

Whether it's designing normalized database schemas, optimizing complex queries, building automated data pipelines, or developing RESTful APIs, I thrive on solving complex technical challenges that drive business value. I'm committed to continuous learning and staying current with emerging technologies in the data and backend engineering ecosystem.
